Fuga de Berlim EP

2017-01-18

in music.

tags: musicbandcamp

I'm pleased to announce (or document, as I don't expect anyone to read this) my Fuga de Berlim EP. It contains 4 tracks in various styles ranging from ambient, deep house influenced samba, a dark breed of electronica and some sort of tropical spoken word synth-folk. For those curious, the voice on the last track is an excerpt from an interview with Jean Baudrillard, where he's talking about his 2007 book Carnival and Cannibal.

Listen and download it at bandcamp.
